Inside Minimalissimo

Magazine

Minimalissimo is an independent print and digital magazine dedicated to minimalism in design. We showcase a curated selection of the finest examples in architecture, interiors, furniture, and homewares. We publish frequent creator stories, always aiming to gain meaningful insights into design processes and outcomes. More importantly, we aim to inspire other creatives to adopt a simple and minimal aesthetic, and to help make a valuable impact on the world through better design.
